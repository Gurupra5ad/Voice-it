Our Story

  • Mission
  • Vision
  • Ideas
Here at Voice-IT, we believe that even apart, it is important to stay connected. We strive to connect everybody with the help of our enthusiastic shows and by providing an innovative platform to showcase talents. By conducting radio shows of multiple languages, we believe we can make it enjoyable and welcome more people into our community. We focus on helping out students academically through events like ‘Mail Time’ and ‘Breaking the Placement Code’. Common Interests are the best example to bring people together and we do that through talent showcases. We motivate people to participate and display their talents confidently. We also aim to create a convivial space where everybody can unapologetically have fun and enjoy being the happiest version of themselves.

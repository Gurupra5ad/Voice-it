VITC's first radio channel

Who we are?

The beginning of Voice-IT was nothing else but a very absurd idea of bringing together students around Chennai who wanted to take part in fests and seminars that were being conducted in universities . However they weren’t able to as they weren’t aware. Voice-IT was an attempt to bring people from different courses and streams together and to provide a platform which would provide equal opportunities to individuals of various educational and cultural backgrounds.

Voice-IT was formed with the prime vision of being the most valuable, vital and vibrant service and to engage the audience and communities of our campus with some enthralling events. We aim at creating exposure among students of VIT Chennai through the college radio and bring everything under one roof. Our agenda is to create a common base for our students with the outer world and other autonomous colleges. We announce daily updates and make sure our students up-to-date with happening news. This helps to open a wide spectrum of technical, cultural and entertainment events for the students and make us an external student representative body. By creating a bridge for external event participation by our students in other colleges, we look forward to expanding student relationships on a large scale.

Our motto is “Feel the Rhythm”. Staying true to the motto, our plan of action is sweet and simple. We collect data from different sources and telecast it on the channel. We acknowledge students, including the advertisements of the events and clubs. We entertain the people of our campus, including residents and make them aware of external events and platforms and ensure they enroll in them. We ensure that we bring out the creativity in each and every individual and motivate them to be the best version of themselves.

Our Story

  • Mission
  • Vision
Here at Voice-IT, we believe that even apart, it is important to stay connected. We strive to connect everybody with the help of our enthusiastic shows and by providing an innovative platform to showcase talents. By conducting radio shows of multiple languages, we believe we can make it enjoyable and welcome more people into our community. We focus on helping out students academically through events like ‘Mail Time’ and ‘Breaking the Placement Code’. Common Interests are the best example to bring people together and we do that through talent showcases. We motivate people to participate and display their talents confidently. We also aim to create a convivial space where everybody can unapologetically have fun and enjoy being the happiest version of themselves.
We aspire to be a community radio for the students of VIT Chennai to create exposure and unite under one roof. We hope to create a common base for the students not only within the college, but also with the outer world and other autonomous colleges. We keep the students up to date on all kinds of news and entertain them with our unique creations along the way. We aim to cater to every one of our students and cover a wide array of interests and concepts, which we hope to accomplish with our diverse range of content in five different languages. We hope to be a representative body of our students and be a bridge of communication for them.
